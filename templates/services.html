Dry Ice Blasting



As trailblazers in our industry, Lee Decorating Inc. proudly distinguishes itself as one of the select few offering the cutting-edge service of ice blasting. Also known as dry ice or CO2 blasting, this revolutionary cleaning method employs accelerated dry ice pellets propelled by compressed air to expertly expunge contaminants from surfaces. Beyond its unrivaled effectiveness, ice blasting stands out for being non-abrasive, non-conductive, residue-free (unlike water or sand blasting), environmentally friendly, and a proven downtime reducer. Ideal for rejuvenating kitchen equipment, revitalizing damaged steel, and revitalizing manufacturing facilities, this process sets the standard for precision and environmental responsibility.
