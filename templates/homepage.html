About Us

A Legacy of Excellence

For over 30 years, Lee Decorating Inc has been providing painting and wall covering finishing services in Northeast USA. Lee Decorating Inc. has completed hundreds of unique and challenging projects in Pennsylvania and Northeast USA.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide high-quality results in painting, wall covering, and finishing services. We are passionate about our work. From commercial, residential, and industrial work, we have the expertise to handle all aspects of these services. Our goal is to deliver high-standard services for any size construction projects in the Northeast United States.
At Lee Decorating Inc, what makes us unique is that we provide the most exceptional workmanship at a competitive rate. Thanks to our years of experience, we deliver an outstanding finished product that leaves a lasting impression on our customers.
