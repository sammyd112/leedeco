A Legacy of Excellence


For more than a quarter-century, Lee Decorating Inc. has been a cornerstone of excellence, delivering unparalleled painting and wall covering finishing services across the United States. Our legacy is etched in the completion of hundreds of distinctive and challenging projects, leaving a mark of craftsmanship and distinction throughout the country.






Our Mission

With a commitment to innovation, sustainability, and personalized service, we aspire to inspire creativity, enhance aesthetics, and leave a lasting impression on every surface we touch. Our mission is to be the trusted partner in color, delivering not just paint, but a vibrant expression of individuality, quality, and enduring beauty to homes and spaces around the world.
